HDFC Life Insurance




When Covid-19 started things turned topsy-turvy, most analysts listed life insurers as disruption-proof. But even they seem to have taken a knock, going by May premium collection data.

HDFC life is up 52% since March 23 but down 17% for the year.

The crisis has undoubtedly brought new challenges,but has also thrown up apportunities but the life insurance industry has been quick to adopt and bring their business back on track.
